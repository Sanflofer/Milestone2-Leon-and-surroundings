If you are visiting the beautiful city of León, and have some spare time, do not hesitate to visit any of these fantastic places

León

León is a small city in the north of Spain, well known for its tapas and beautiful architecture. It is a place that will delight and enchant all visitors. León certainly has a lot to offer. To find out more about its attractions and places of interes, please clickhere

Ponferrada

Ponferrada is one of the main stopovers in the Pilgrim's Walk to Santiago de Compostela. Here you will find a grand medival castle and many places to wine and dine.

Astorga

A Roman wall city providing santury to one of Gaudi's most prominent architectural designs. A visit to the cathedral must not go amiss as well as indulging in local honey soup pastries names Hojaldres.

More about Astorga

Las Médulas

An awe-inspiring landscape fornmed by the Roman explotation of gold. This World Heritage site will provide you with plenty of historic knowledge and walking routes to explore.

More about Las Médulas

Valporquero caves

Ancient caves and georges in the mountains of León. It is great for those who enjoy hiking, climbing and potholing. Come and admire the magnificent stalactites and stalagmites.
